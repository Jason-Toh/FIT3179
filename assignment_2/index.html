Crimes in New York City

There are many types of crimes that exists in New York City. Some can be mild which the offender only need to pay a fine while there are others which are very serious that can be land in life imprisonment or even the death penalty.

This crimes can be categorised into violent crime or property crime. This visualisation will provide insight on the many types of crime that occur in NYC.

Location of Crimes

Based on the choropleth map shown on the left, Brookyln is seen as the hotspot area for crimes to occur. However, part of the reason can be attributed to the fact that it is the most populated borough with a population of 2.5 million people.

Staten island's crime rate is the lowest. However, it does have a population of 470k. Therefore, one should not look just the number of crime but also at the general population at that vicinity.

Most frequent crime over the years

Out of all the crimes in NYC, we can see that Property theft has taken the first spot in most frequent crime with a whopping 29%, followed by Larceny with 24%, Murder with 19% and Rape with 18%. The least frequent crime is a three way tie between Arson, Harrassment and Kidnapping with each being 3%.

Gender, age and race

Does crime have a distinction between age, gender and race? We see that most frequent age group that likely commits a crime lies in 25-44 age group. Another observation can be seen that Males are more likely to commit crimes than females across all the age groups. More than half of the crimes are reported to be Black which has 53% overall. The second largest demographic race is the white hispanic with 25%. The smallest demographic race to commit a crime are the Asians which has 4%.
